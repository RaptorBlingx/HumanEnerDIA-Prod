About HumanEnerDIA

HumanEnerDIA (Human-Centric Intelligent Energy Management System with Digital Intelligent Assistant) is a comprehensive, production-ready solution designed for industrial facilities to monitor, analyze, and optimize their energy consumption in real-time. HumanEnerDIA includes Digital Intelligent Assistance (DIA) interfaces: an OVOS voice assistant for hands-free queries and control over EnMS, and an AI chatbot that supports users with Energy Management System (EnMS) know-how and platform guidance.

Mission

Our mission is to empower organizations with actionable insights into their energy usage, enabling them to reduce costs, improve efficiency, and meet sustainability goals while maintaining full compliance with ISO 50001 standards. Through DIA, we add voice (OVOS) and an AI chatbot to make EnMS guidance and day-to-day actions faster and easier.

Key Features

Real-Time Monitoring

Live energy consumption data from industrial equipment with millisecond precision.

AI-Powered Analytics

Machine learning models for anomaly detection, forecasting, and baseline predictions.

Advanced Dashboards

Interactive Grafana dashboards with customizable KPIs and visualizations.

Smart Alerts

Automated notifications for anomalies, threshold breaches, and maintenance needs.

ISO 50001 Support

Built-in reporting and documentation aligned with ISO 50001 energy management principles.

Voice Integration

OVOS voice assistant for hands-free data queries and system control.

Architecture

ENMS Platform is built on a modern microservices architecture with:

  • TimescaleDB: Time-series database optimized for IoT data
  • FastAPI: High-performance Python APIs for analytics and queries
  • Node-RED: Visual data pipeline for MQTT to database ETL
  • Grafana: Professional-grade visualization and dashboards
  • Redis: Real-time caching and pub/sub messaging
  • Docker: Containerized deployment for scalability
  • DIA Interfaces: OVOS voice assistant and an EnMS expert chatbot for guidance, queries, and workflow support.

Technology Stack

Our platform leverages cutting-edge technologies including Python, PostgreSQL, React, Docker, MQTT, and machine learning libraries (scikit-learn, Prophet, ARIMA) to deliver enterprise-grade energy management capabilities.

Environmental Impact

By providing detailed insights into energy consumption patterns and identifying optimization opportunities, ENMS Platform helps organizations significantly reduce their carbon footprint and contribute to global sustainability goals.

Social Impact

By adding Digital Intelligent Assistance (DIA) to Intel50001, HumanEnerDIA lowers the skills and effort needed to run an Energy Management System—helping workers interact with complex energy data more easily and reducing the technical burden on operational users.

Through OVOS voice support and a text-based assistant connected to the EnMS knowledge base, the solution supports learning (standards, procedures, and practical EnMS know-how), increases engagement, and is designed to improve day-to-day usability and wellbeing in factory roles.

We also plan webinars/workshops to demonstrate real-world usage and broaden uptake among manufacturing SMEs.
