HumanEnerDIA

ISO 50001 Framework Support

HumanEnerDIA is designed to help SMEs implement energy management practices aligned with ISO 50001:2018 - the international standard for Energy Management Systems. Our platform supports awareness and implementation of ISO 50001 principles.

✓ Built on ISO 50001 Principles

Our platform provides all the tools, documentation, and processes needed to establish, implement, maintain, and improve your energy management system in accordance with ISO 50001 requirements.

Key ISO 50001 Requirements Coverage

Energy Review

Comprehensive energy data collection, analysis, and identification of Significant Energy Uses (SEUs)

Energy Baseline

Automated baseline establishment using ML models and historical data analysis

Performance Indicators

Real-time EnPIs (Energy Performance Indicators) tracking and visualization

Objectives & Targets

Energy objective setting, tracking, and progress monitoring

Action Plans

Energy action plan management with task tracking and resource allocation

Monitoring & Measurement

Continuous monitoring of energy consumption, EnPIs, and action plan effectiveness

Internal Audit

Audit trail, compliance reports, and documentation for management reviews

Continual Improvement

Anomaly detection, opportunity identification, and improvement tracking
